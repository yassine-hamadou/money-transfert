FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS.

How do I get my CVC code?

When you register, you Card Verification Code is shown with your account number and password.

Can I change my password?

Yes!

How does BornaSend work?

You sign up, go to an agent to fund your account, and you can start making transactions. If you what to withdraw, you simply apply and wait for approval.

Why do I have to provide my information?

We require you to provide us with your information in order to provide you with the best services. We require you to provide us with, as a minimum, your full legal name, date of birth, address and nationality in order to provide you with the Remittance Services.

How are my information protected?

Protecting your personal data is very important to us. BornaSend maintains physical, electronic and procedural safeguards that comply with the applicable laws to protect your personal information from unauthorised access.
